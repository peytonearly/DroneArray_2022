Processor:
- Raspberry Pi 3 B+
- Raspberry Pi 4 B (2 or 4 GB)

Motor:
- Mudder Micro Electric Motor (https://www.amazon.com/Mudder-Electric-23000RPM-Rectangular-Projects/dp/B08ZHV7PCB/)
    - Rated for 10,000 rpm, maximum 23,000 rpm
    - 20mm width x 38 mm length
    - 3 V
- AutoToolHome Mini DC Motor (https://www.amazon.com/AUTOTOOLHOME-Torque-Traxxas-Wheels-Electric/dp/B01M58POHF/)
    - > 2 in length
    - 12 V
    - 12,000 rpm

Sensors:
- BMP180 Barometer
    - Air pressure
    - Temperature
- GP2Y0A02YK
    - Infrared distance meter (20-150 cm range)
- GPS NEO-6M Module
    - GPS receiver
    - COmpatible with "minicom" and "gpsd" raspberry pi packages
- MPU-6050 Gyroscope
    - 3-axis gyroscope
    - Accelerometer
- 433 MHz Set
    - 433 MHz radio transmitter and receiver
- PCA9685 Servo Board
    - Control multiple motors per board
- MCP3008 ADC
    - Analog to digital converter (Raspberry Pi does not have analog IO pins)
- Relais
    - Voltage relay
- LM2596 Buck Converter
    - Regulate voltages to not exceed 5 V
